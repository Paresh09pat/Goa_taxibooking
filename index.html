How to online car booking

  1. First by downloading Quickzet Trip application from play Store or App Store.
  2. Create your own account on the application
  3. Click on the search bar to go to the name of the Place
  4. Select the car you want.
  5. After Clicking on proceed, the driver will connect and the OTP Screen will appear.
  6. After the OTP Screen opens, the driver will bring he car to you within a short time.
  7. Once your ride is complete, you can pay online or offline.

How you can register your car with the company and earn money

  1. First by downloading Quickzet Driver application from play Store or App Store.
  2. Once your enter the application, select the online option.
  3. Accept the passenger's request or pass it to another driver
  4. After accepting the request, you will see the passenger's location and contact details.
  5. When the passenger meets you, ask for the OTP from him
  6. Collect the payment from the passenger after the right is completed.
